PPTS: Custom Appointments Notification

In Odoo PPTS: Custom Appointments Notification module used to create and customize notification(Mail) as per customer and attendees and therapist needs.

Step 1:

In Appointment Module click on Configuration -> Notification -> Appointment Notification to create new appointment notification.

Step 2:

After Creating new notification record, at first Notification Active Button is used to Activate. If it is deactivate the notification cannot send to any customers or therapist.

Step 3:

If Notification is Active, there are twelve type of Appointment notification tilte in every notification title there is a activate button

  • Activate button
  • Mailing list
  • Mail Template
  • Whatsapp Template

Step 4:

The Mailing List has been created from the Email Marketing -> Mailing List -> Create a new mailing list with mailing list contacts and Appointment mail template and whatsapp template.

Step 5:

Notifications

Appointment Notifications Description
Appointment Creation The Notifiction will send to the customer on creating the appointment with the given mail Creation template.
If whatsapp template also selected, the notification will send through mail.
On Selecting the mailing list, the notification is also send to Mailing list contacts.
Appointment Approval The Notification will send to the customer on approval of appointment booked as per the selected Approve Customer template.
If whatsapp template also selected, the notification will send through mail.
On Selecting the mailing list, the notification is also send to Mailing list contacts.
Appointment Cancel The Notification will send to the customer on cancelling the appointment booked.
If whatsapp template also selected, the notification will send through mail.
On Selecting the mailing list, the notification is also send to Mailing list contacts.
Appointment Reschedule The Notification will send to the customer on rescheduling the appointment booked by the customer or user for som emergency reasons.
If whatsapp template also selected, the notification will send through mail.
On Selecting the mailing list, the notification is also send to Mailing list contacts.
Need to notify the staff if any faciliator has not created their availiblity This Notification will alert the faciliator to create availability by Mail and Whatsapp on that day.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts, it only available for staff and faciliator.
Need to notify the facilitator twice a day Once in the morning + evening ) till they don't complete their availablity This Notification will alert facilitator through mail and whatsapp twice a day in morning and evening.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts, it only available for staff and faciliator.
Rooms Allocation The Notification will send to the customer on allocating room for the appointment booked.
In this, the mail will send to mailing list contacts,if Mailing list has been selected.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
Attendees Reminder mail before 2 Hours The Notification will send to the attendees before 2 Hours appointment need to attend.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
Attendees Reminder mail before 1 day The Notification will send to the attendees before 1 day appointment need to attend.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
Attendees after attended the Appointments - Thanks The Thanks Notification will send to the attendees after appointment attended.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
Attendees Payment Reminder This Notification will send to the attendees after appointment completed to remain the payment.
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
Invoice & Payment If the payment/invoice is paid it will send notification for payment completed status
In this, the mail would not send to mailing list contacts.
it will send mail through mail and whatsapp to the customer and staff.
